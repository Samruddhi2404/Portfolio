👩‍💻 About Me

I’m a multidisciplinary creative with a strong passion for building digital experiences that are both visually engaging and functionally effective.

With hands-on experience in frontend development, graphic design, and social media content creation, I love turning ideas into intuitive, user-centered designs that speak for themselves. Whether it's a responsive website, a branding asset, or an engaging social post — I always strive to deliver polished work that connects with people.

I believe in continuous learning, collaboration, and the power of design to make an impact. If you're looking for someone who can code with creativity and design with purpose — I’d love to connect!
